# ZidRun — Brand Voice, Visual Kit & Asset Guide

Use this as the style guide for everything posted. Consistency here is what makes a new account feel like a real brand.

---

## 1. Brand voice

**Personality:** Energetic, encouraging, local, no-BS. A running buddy who happens to be organized. Proud of Algeria.

**We sound like:** "Allez, on court. Trouve ta course, on s'occupe du reste."
**We don't sound like:** corporate, preachy, elitist, or like a Western fitness brand copy-pasted.

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Speak Darija-friendly French + natural Arabic | Over-formal MSA-only or academic French |
| Celebrate every level (first 5K = hero) | Glorify only fast/elite runners |
| Be useful: tell them exactly what to do | Vague hype with no CTA |
| Use Algerian places, humor, references | Generic "global" stock vibes |
| Short, punchy, emoji-light but warm | Emoji soup / clickbait lies |

**Tone by pillar:** Inspire = warm & emotional · Enable = clear & helpful · Coach = smart & reassuring · Relatable = funny & local · Organizer = professional & confident.

---

## 2. Languages
- **French:** primary for captions (widest comprehension).
- **Arabic:** include AR line for reach; Darija tone OK in captions, cleaner Arabic for value/explainer.
- **English:** lighter, for diaspora + EN-first followers; lead EN on diaspora-angled posts only.
- **Rule:** on-screen video text in ONE language (the post's primary); caption can carry the other two.

## 3. Visual identity

**Logo:** The ZidRun mark is a dynamic **"Z" speed-blade** — a green chevron (top) + orange chevron (bottom) forming a forward-leaning Z that reads as motion/speed — followed by the **"idRun"** wordmark in bold italic ink. The Z can stand alone as an app icon/avatar.

- **Primary green:** `#8BC53F` (energy, "go", the top blade)
- **Accent orange:** `#F47A20` (warmth, the bottom blade)
- **Ink (text/wordmark):** `#141618` (near-black)
- **Off-white:** `#FAFAFA`

**Logo files (in repo):**
- `public/zidrun-logo.svg` — full color, light backgrounds
- `public/zidrun-logo-dark.svg` — for dark backgrounds (green/orange mark, light wordmark)
- `public/zidrun-logo-race.svg` — flashy "race" variant
- `public/zidrun-logo.png` — raster fallback
- `brand-explore/export/` — additional exports (mark-only, shiny, dark)

**Logo usage rules:**
- Keep clear space around the mark; never stretch, recolor, or rotate.
- On busy photos, use the dark variant or a mono/white version with the Z mark only.
- App icon / profile avatar = the **Z mark alone** (green+orange) on white or ink.
- Never put the green blade on a green background or orange on orange — keep contrast.

- **Typography (graphics):** bold geometric/italic sans for headlines to echo the wordmark's forward lean (e.g. Montserrat/Poppins italic); clean sans for body.
- **Photo style:** real Algerian runners, real cities, golden-hour, motion, sweat, finish-line joy. Avoid sterile stock.
- **Template motifs:** the Z speed-blade, diagonal speed lines, route/map lines, distance tags (5K/10K/21K), wilaya tags. Green = action/CTA, orange = warmth/highlight.

### Reusable graphic templates to build (Canva)
1. Race card teaser (race name · wilaya · date · distance · price · "S'inscrire").
2. "Course du week-end" roundup (3–5 races grid).
3. Quote/stat card (ink or green bg, orange accent, Z watermark).
4. Carousel cover + inner-slide layout (Z blade motif in corner).
5. Coach tip card.
6. Organizer CTA card.

## 6. UTM / tracking scheme

Tag every outbound link so sign-ups are attributable:
```
https://zidrun.com/races?utm_source={instagram|facebook|tiktok}&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch_2026&utm_content={post-slug}
```
Examples:
- Reveal Reel → `...utm_source=instagram&utm_content=reveal_reel`
- Coach demo TT → `...utm_source=tiktok&utm_content=coach_demo`
- Organizer FB post → `...utm_source=facebook&utm_content=organizer_cta`

Keep a UTM log (sheet): slug · platform · date · link · clicks · sign-ups.

## 7. Compliance & safety notes
- AI coach: always frame as **informational, not medical advice**. Never promise medical outcomes or replace a doctor/physio. Add a soft disclaimer on coach posts: *"Conseils informatifs, pas un avis médical."*
- Use only photos you have rights to (own shoots, organizer-provided with permission, or licensed). Get consent before featuring a runner's face/story.
- Re-export any re-used video **without competitor watermarks**.
- Respect group rules when posting in Facebook running groups — value first, never spam.

---

## 8. Engagement playbook
- Reply to every comment in first 60 min (algorithm + community).
- Turn good comments/DMs into content (Q&A Reels, FAQ).
- Use Stories daily: polls, "this or that" (5K vs 10K), countdowns, UGC re-shares.
- Pin best comment with a CTA.
- DM new followers a friendly welcome + link (no spam).
