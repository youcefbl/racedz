# ZidRun — Social Media Launch Marketing Plan

**Prepared by:** Senior Marketing Manager
**Campaign type:** Launch awareness
**Primary markets:** Algeria (FR + AR), diaspora (EN)
**Channels:** Instagram, Facebook, TikTok
**Planning horizon:** 6-week launch sprint, then evergreen sustain

---

## 1. Executive summary

ZidRun is **a personal AI running coach built for Algeria** — that also happens to be the one place to **discover and register for every race in the country**. Today, Algerian runners train alone with no plan and no feedback, and find races through scattered Facebook posts, WhatsApp groups, and word of mouth. ZidRun gives every runner a coach in their pocket — in Darija-friendly French and Arabic — and a single home for every race.

**Why lead with the coach.** Races are the *hook* (a reason to sign up today), but the AI coach is the *habit* (a reason to open the app every day) and the *moat* (no Facebook group or spreadsheet can copy it). The coach is what makes ZidRun a daily companion instead of a directory people visit twice a year. So this campaign puts the coach at the center and uses races as the on-ramp.

The launch goal is **awareness + activated sign-ups**: make ZidRun the name a runner thinks of the moment they decide "I want to start running / I want to run my first 10K." High-frequency, mostly-organic social push, bilingual-first (FR/AR), video-led (Reels/TikTok), fronted by a **coach persona ("Coach Zid")** and anchored by a founding-community offer.

**North-star for the campaign:** activated sign-ups — account created **+ a coach goal set or first run logged/tracked** (not just a race viewed).

## 2. Product positioning

**One-liner (FR):** ZidRun — Ton coach de course IA dans la poche. Un plan sur mesure, un suivi en direct, et toutes les courses d'Algérie au même endroit.

**One-liner (AR):** ‏ZidRun — كوتش الجري متاعك بالذكاء الاصطناعي، في جيبك. برنامج على قدّك، متابعة مباشرة، وكل سباقات الجزائر في بلاصة وحدة.

**One-liner (EN):** ZidRun — Your AI running coach in your pocket. A personalized plan, live run tracking, and every race in Algeria in one place.

**Short catchphrase (all langs):** **Zid! 🏃** ("keep going / push" in Darija) — the coach's rallying cry and the brand's verb.

### Positioning statement
For **anyone in Algeria who wants to start running or run a better race**, **ZidRun** is a **personal AI running coach** that **builds your plan, tracks your runs live, gives you feedback after every run, and connects you to every race in the country** — all in Darija-friendly French and Arabic. Unlike training alone with a generic Western app (or a Facebook group), ZidRun is personalized, local, trilingual, and free to start.

### Value pillars (coach-led)
1. **Train — your AI coach** *(hero)* — a personalized weekly plan built from your level, goal, and history; adjusts as you run.
2. **Track — live during your run** — real-time average & current pace, per-km splits, then instant post-run AI feedback.
3. **Progress — never guess again** — see your trend vs your goal, get profile-matched tips (beginner, marathon, coming-back, heavier-runner), ask the coach anything.
4. **Discover & register** — every race (5K, 10K, semi, marathon, trail) filterable by wilaya/distance/date/price, online sign-up in minutes — the reason to lace up.
5. **Local & trilingual** — built for Algeria, in Arabic, French, and English (RTL-native), by runners here.
6. **For organizers** — publish a race and manage participants without spreadsheets (supply side).

## 5. Campaign strategy & big idea

### Big idea: **"Zid! — L'Algérie qui court, avec un coach dans la poche"**
A movement *with a guide*. We celebrate the growing running culture in Algeria — every wilaya, every level — and give it a face: **Coach Zid**, ZidRun's coaching persona whose catchphrase *"Zid!"* (زيد — "keep going / push") is both encouragement and the brand's verb. Every post either **coaches** (teaches, motivates, shows the plan working), **inspires** (the movement), or **enables** (find/register). The coach is the through-line that ties every post to a person, not a logo.

### Three creative angles on rotation
1. **Coach** *(lead)* — "Coach Zid" tips & voiceovers, "7 days with my AI coach", live-run/split screen-recordings, before/after a training block, "the coach told me to slow down — here's why", answer-a-DM Q&A.
2. **Inspire** — runner stories, sunrise runs in Algerian cities, "from couch to 5K", finish-line emotion.
3. **Enable + relatable** — how to find/register for a race in 60s; Algerian humor ("POV: tu cherches une course sur Facebook depuis 2h"), city pride, Darija captions, organizer how-to.

### Launch beats
- **Tease** (Week 1): "something is coming for Algerian runners."
- **Reveal** (Week 2): what ZidRun is + founding-community CTA.
- **Prove** (Weeks 3–4): product demos, coach, first races live.
- **Sustain** (Weeks 5–6+): UGC, race countdowns, organizer spotlights.

## 6. Channel plan

### Instagram (brand hub — visual + community)
- **Formats:** Reels (lead format, 4–5/wk), carousels (educational/value, 2/wk), Stories daily (polls, countdowns, behind-the-scenes), occasional static.
- **Role:** brand identity, community building, saves & shares.
- **Tactics:** geo-tags by wilaya, collab posts with running clubs/influencers, Stories link to sign-up, highlight reels per pillar.

### Facebook (reach + organizers + events)
- **Formats:** native video (re-cut Reels), link posts, image posts, **Facebook Events** for each race, posting into Algerian running **Groups**.
- **Role:** widest reach in Algeria, older + organizer audience, race events, community groups.
- **Tactics:** seed value posts into existing running/marathon groups (non-spammy, value-first), create a ZidRun page + group, list every race as an FB Event linking back to ZidRun.

### TikTok (organic growth engine — younger runners)
- **Formats:** short native video (7–25s), trend-jacking with running spin, POV/relatable, quick how-tos, "running in [city]" series.
- **Role:** fast organic reach, top-of-funnel, younger first-time runners.
- **Tactics:** trending Algerian sounds, hook in first 1s, 1 clear idea per video, post 1–2×/day, reply to comments with video.

**Cross-posting rule:** shoot vertical video once, cut for Reels + TikTok; never post a visible competitor watermark (re-export clean). Tailor captions per platform/language.

## 10. Acquisition funnel & conversion

```
Awareness (Reels/TikTok/FB reach)
   → Interest (carousel/value + profile visit)
      → Click (link in bio / Stories link / FB Event)
         → Sign-up (account created)
            → Activation (view a race / set a coach goal)
               → Retention (registration, coach plan, follow club)
                  → Advocacy (share finish, UGC, refer a friend)
```

- **Link-in-bio:** single landing, **coach button first** — *Try your AI coach free — 7 days* · *Find a race* · *Publish a race (organizers)*.
- **CTA per post type:** Coach → "try your coach free / comment ZID and I'll DM you the link"; Inspire → follow; Enable → "link in bio"; Organizer → "publish free".
- **Auto-DM funnel:** on coach posts, "comment **ZID**" → auto-DM the coach link (ManyChat, see tools doc). Turns comments into sign-ups and boosts reach.
- **Founding offer:** "Founding Runner" badge / early-access + an **extended coach trial or discounted first year** for the first 1,000 sign-ups to drive urgency (see §10b).
- **Activation nudge:** the moment someone signs up, get them to **set a goal or log one run** — that's the coach's aha and our real activation metric (§4).

## 10b. Monetization, trial & conversion strategy

**Model: freemium + reverse trial.** Tracking is the *habit* (open the app daily), the AI coach is the *premium*. So the core stays free forever and the coach is what people pay for — but we never hard-wall someone who just tasted it.

### The three tiers

| Tier | What they get | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Free forever** | GPS tracking, run logging, history, per-km splits, photos, race discovery & registration | The daily habit + the hook. Keeps everyone in the app. |
| **Coach trial — 7 days** | Full AI coach (plan, live feedback, unlimited-feel usage) from the moment they set a goal | The "I'd pay for this" moment, at peak motivation |
| **Free after trial (reverse trial)** | Coach drops to a *small* monthly allowance (~5 coach actions/mo) — **not** a lockout | Missing the premium they tasted → the subscribe |
| **Subscribed** | Full coach (daily/monthly high limits), push **annual** with a discount | The revenue |

### Why 7 days, not 30
Our current **1-month free is too long**: the novelty fades before we ask, people extract a whole training block for free, and — critically in Algeria, where paying means a manual proof (CIB/Edahabia/CCP/Baridimob) — we delay the payment decision until motivation has cooled. **Shorter trial = we ask while they're still excited.** 7 days is the mobile-subscription sweet spot; the reverse-trial safety net (limited free coach, not a wall) is what actually lifts conversion.

### What comparable apps do
| App | Trial | Model |
|---|---|---|
| **Runna** (closest — AI running plans) | **7 days** | Trial → paid, no free tier |
| Strava | ~14–30 days | Generous free-forever + premium trial |
| Nike Run Club | — | Fully free |
| Fitbod (AI workouts) | **3 free workouts** | Usage-metered, not time |
| Headspace / Calm (habit) | 7–14 days | Freemium + trial |

### Marketing implications (what changes in our messaging)
- **CTA everywhere becomes "Try your AI coach free — 7 days"** (was open-ended "free"). Adds a clean, honest urgency we can put on every coach post and the link-in-bio.
- **Trial-ending nudge is a content + lifecycle beat:** in-app + email/push "2 days left with Coach Zid" → prompt to subscribe right after a great run or first PB (peak-motivation ask).
- **Founding Runner offer (§10) ties in here:** first 1,000 get an *extended trial (e.g. 14 days) or discounted annual* — real urgency, not a vague badge.
- **Never advertise "free forever coach"** — free-forever is the *tracking*; the coach is the paid hero. Keep the two clearly separated in copy so we don't train people to expect premium for free.
- **Price/plan reveal:** keep pricing out of top-of-funnel awareness posts; surface it at the trial-end and on the pricing screen. Lead with value, ask at the moment of value.

> Product note: the tier machinery already exists (trial window + per-tier coach limits). This section is the *marketing* stance; the product change is (a) trial length → 7 days and (b) post-trial keeps a small free coach allowance instead of zero.

## 12. Risks & mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Thin supply (few races) at launch | Onboard 10–20 organizers BEFORE reveal week; show real races day one |
| Trilingual workload | FR primary; AR for reach; EN lighter (diaspora). Templates in `posts/` |
| Low video output capacity | Batch-shoot monthly; repurpose 1 shoot into 5–8 clips |
| Coach health-advice sensitivity | Never make medical claims; "informational, not medical advice" disclaimer; defer to pros |
| Paid spend with no creative proof | Organic-first; only boost proven posts |
| Seasonality (summer heat, Ramadan) | Lean into "early-morning / evening run" content; Ramadan-aware messaging |
