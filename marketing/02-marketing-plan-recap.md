# ZidRun — One-Page Launch Recap

**Product:** ZidRun — find, register for & manage running races in Algeria + AI running coach.
**Campaign:** Launch awareness · **Languages:** FR + AR + EN · **Channels:** Instagram · Facebook · TikTok · **Sprint:** 6 weeks.

## Big idea
**"Zid! — L'Algérie qui court, avec un coach dans la poche"** — a movement *with a guide*, fronted by **Coach Zid** (*"Zid!"* = زيد, "keep going"). Every post **coaches**, **inspires**, or **enables**. The coach is the through-line.

## Positioning
**A personal AI running coach built for Algeria** — personalized plan, live run tracking (pace + splits), post-run feedback, profile-matched tips — that's *also* the one place to find & register for every race. Coach = habit + moat; races = hook. Trilingual, local, free to start.

## Audiences
Aspiring runners (18–35) · active amateurs (25–45) · race organizers (B2B) · diaspora (EN).

## Funnel
Reach (Reels/TikTok) → Profile/value (carousels) → Click (link-in-bio: *Find a race · AI coach · Publish a race*) → Sign-up → Activation (view race / set coach goal) → UGC/referral.

## Monetization (freemium + reverse trial)
Tracking + race discovery **free forever** (the habit) · AI coach = **7-day trial** (was 1 month — too long) · after trial, coach drops to a **small free allowance, not a lockout** (reverse trial) → subscribe, push **annual**. Ask at peak motivation (trial-end + after a great run). CTA: *"Try your AI coach free — 7 days."* Full detail in `00 §10b`.

## Launch beats
Wk1 **Tease** → Wk2 **Reveal** (+ Founding Runner offer) → Wk3 **Discover** → Wk4 **Coach** → Wk5 **UGC** → Wk6 **Milestone**.

## Content mix (coach-led)
🤖 **Coach 35% (hero)** · 🏃 Inspire 25% · 🔍 Enable 20% · 😄 Relatable 12% · 📣 Organizer 8%.
Cadence: IG 7/wk + daily Stories · FB 5/wk + Events + groups · TikTok 7–10/wk.

## KPIs (6-week targets)
Reach 150k–300k · +3–5k followers · ER ≥5% (IG/FB) / ≥8% (TT) · **1,000–2,500 sign-ups** · ≥50% activation · 15–30 organizer races.

## Budget (lean, organic-first)
~45k–145k DZD/mo: boost proven posts + 3–6 micro-influencers + in-house phone video. Only pay to amplify winners.

## Top risks → mitigations
Thin race supply → onboard 10–20 organizers before reveal · trilingual load → FR primary, templates ready · coach claims → "informational, not medical advice."

## First 5 actions
1. Claim 3 profiles + **coach-first** link-in-bio.
2. **Decide the Coach Zid persona route** (real / AI avatar / hybrid) — unblocks all coach video.
3. Onboard 10–20 organizers (real races for reveal day).
4. Screen-record the coach once (goal, live run + splits, feedback, tip, chat) + set up ManyChat "comment ZID → DM link".
5. Ship Week 1 Tease → Week 2 Reveal, coach as the hero pillar.
