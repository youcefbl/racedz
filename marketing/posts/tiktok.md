## TikTok growth rules
- **Hook is everything:** state the payoff or tension in the first second; no slow intros/logos.
- **One idea per video.** Don't cram features — make separate clips.
- **Native, not ad:** shoot on phone, real runners, real cities. Polished ads underperform here.
- **Trending sounds:** check the Algeria TikTok trends weekly; add a running spin.
- **Caption on screen** in the primary language (most watch muted).
- **Post 1–2×/day**, reply to comments fast, and turn comments into the next videos.
- **Series win:** "Run in [city]", "Reply-to-comment", "60s registration" — recurring formats train the algorithm + audience.
- **Duets/Stitch:** invite users to duet their finish-line → fuels #JeCoursAvecZidRun UGC.
