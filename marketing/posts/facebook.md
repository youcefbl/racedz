## 📌 Facebook Groups playbook (high-leverage, low-cost)
1. **Find & join** active Algerian running/marathon/sport groups (search "course Algérie", "running DZ", "semi-marathon", city running clubs).
2. **Read the rules** — many ban promo. Earn trust first: answer questions, share genuinely useful tips for ~1 week before posting links.
3. **Value-first posts:** "Voici toutes les courses de [ville] ce mois 👇" (helpful list) — link to ZidRun naturally, not as an ad.
4. **Engage** with members' posts; reply to "anyone know a race in X?" with the ZidRun link.
5. **Partner with admins** — offer to feature their club's races for free.
6. **Never spam** the same copy across groups — tailor each, space them out.
