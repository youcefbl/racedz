A running watch turns "I went for a run" into real data: pace, distance, heart rate, and a record you can actually improve on. You don't need the most expensive model — you need the right one for how you train. Here are the picks that make sense in Algeria, from cheapest to premium.

<Callout type="tip" title="The one feature that matters most">
**Built-in GPS.** A watch with its own GPS tracks your route and distance without your phone. Cheaper smartbands borrow your phone's GPS instead — fine to start, but you'll want the real thing once you're chasing a time.
</Callout>
